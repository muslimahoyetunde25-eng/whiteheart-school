Our Programs

Reception Discovery

Designed for children aged 3-5 years, our Reception program focuses on foundational skills through play-based learning. Children explore concepts of numbers, letters, shapes, colors, and basic science through carefully prepared materials and activities.

Nursery

Building on Reception, our Nursery program introduces more structured learning while maintaining the playful approach. Children learn about nature, culture, history, and geography through hands-on experiences and collaborative projects.

Kindergarten

Our Kindergarten program prepares children for formal schooling with a focus on language arts, mathematics, science, and social studies. Children develop critical thinking skills and learn to work independently and collaboratively.

Primary 1-5

Our Primary program continues to build on the Montessori foundation with specialized learning areas including practical life skills, sensorial exploration, mathematical operations, language arts, cultural studies, and artistic expression.
