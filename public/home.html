Our Story

Founded in 2015, Whiteheart School (Montessori) is dedicated to nurturing young learners through a child-centered approach that promotes independence, creativity, and excellence.

Our experienced teachers guide children from Reception to Primary with love and dedication, combining Montessori principles with modern teaching techniques to help each child reach their full potential academically, socially, and emotionally.

Our Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To provide a nurturing Montessori environment that fosters curiosity, creativity, and academic excellence while developing moral values and confidence in every child.

Our Vision

To be recognized as a leading Montessori school, shaping young minds into compassionate, responsible, and innovative future leaders.
