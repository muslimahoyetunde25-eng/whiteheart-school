About Us

Whiteheart School (Montessori) is committed to nurturing young learners through a child-centered approach that promotes independence, creativity, and excellence.

Our curriculum is designed to develop critical thinking, social skills, and a lifelong love of learning. We believe every child has unique talents and we provide opportunities to explore and grow in a supportive environment.

From early childhood to preparatory levels, Whiteheart School focuses on holistic development—academics, arts, sports, and character formation—ensuring students are well-prepared for the future.

Our Mission & Vision

Mission: To provide a nurturing, creative, and academically stimulating environment that encourages children to reach their full potential.

Vision: To be a leading Montessori school recognized for academic excellence, innovation, and the holistic development of our students.
