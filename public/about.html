About Whiteheart School

Our Story

Founded in 2010, Whiteheart School began with a vision to provide quality Montessori education rooted in compassion and respect. Our founding principles emphasize holistic development, fostering an environment where children can thrive academically, socially, and emotionally.

Our Philosophy

We believe in nurturing the whole child by providing a balanced approach to learning that combines traditional academic subjects with creative arts, practical life skills, and character development. Our teachers are trained Montessori educators who understand the unique needs of each child.

Our Commitment

At Whiteheart, we are committed to creating a safe, loving, and supportive learning environment where every child feels valued and respected. We focus on developing critical thinking, problem-solving skills, and a lifelong love of learning.
